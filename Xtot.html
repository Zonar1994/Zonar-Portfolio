Personal Project Documentation: Enhancing Large Language Models with ToT

Introduction

I embarked on my personal project with the aim of addressing the limitations of current large language models, focusing on enhancing their capabilities in strategic reasoning and complex decision-making. My approach was to delve into and apply the "Tree of Thoughts" methodology. To structure my efforts, I utilized the Double Diamond method, a strategy that encompasses four critical stages of development. So far, I have successfully navigated through the initial phases of discovery and definition, where I laid the groundwork by researching and outlining the project's scope. Currently, I am in the midst of the development phase, actively working on implementing and testing the ToT methodology.

Discover Phase: Laying the Foundations

In the initial Discover phase, I focused on broad exploration. This stage was about opening up to various possibilities and understanding the broader context of large language models and the "Tree of Thoughts" methodology. My activities included extensive research to gather a wide range of information and perspectives and formulating "How Might We" questions to think more broadly about potential applications and implications of the ToT methodology.

Research Questions

  • How might we integrate the Tree of Thoughts methodology into AI systems to improve their strategic decision-making and problem-solving abilities?
  • How might we measure and evaluate the effectiveness of the Tree of Thoughts methodology in enhancing the problem-solving capabilities of AI models?
  • How might we ensure that the integration of the Tree of Thoughts methodology in AI aligns with ethical standards and positively impacts society?
  • Projectplan:

    • Comparative Analysis A&B Testing The project plan meticulously outlines the strategic approach and structured timeline for exploring and enhancing large language models using the Tree of Thoughts methodology.

    Define Phase: Narrowing Down

    Moving into the Define phase, I aimed to narrow down the focus based on the insights gained from the Discover phase. This was about creating a clear project definition and setting specific objectives. I refined the scope and distilled the information gathered to define the specific areas of focus for my project, deciding on the aspects of the ToT methodology I would concentrate on and how it could be applied to improve LLMs.

  • Comparative Analysis | A&B Testing

    • The project includes a comprehensive comparative analysis, using A&B testing methods, to evaluate different AI prompting methodologies and their effectiveness in various problem-solving scenarios.

  • Tict Research

    • The Tict Research aspect of the project delves into understanding the societal impact of advanced AI technologies, specifically assessing how the integration of new methodologies like Tree of Thoughts affects various social and ethical dimensions.
