About Me

Digital Creator

My name is Zonar Caldera and I am currently studying Media Design at Fontys IT in Tilburg. I Grew up on a little island in the Caribbean called Curaçao. I have always been fascinated with technology and how it can improve our everyday lives. My hobbies away from the screens are skateboarding, surfing exploring nature! I love the creative process of coming up with new and innovative solutions for complex challenges. Next to that, I enjoy developing and designing the Front-end of a website for the correct end-user base.

What do I want to do in the future?

When I have my bachelor's degree, I would love to develop creative solutions for complex problems.  From programming, the Front-end of a website, designing logos, editing videos, Augmented Reality experiences, or User Experience.
