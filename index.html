Introduction


Welcome to my reading guide for semester six! I'm excited to work on a range of creative projects that will challenge my skills and creativity. This reading guide covers the approach and results of projects that I will be working on this semester.
The semester is divided into three main projects: start-up project, client project, and individual project. During the first six weeks, I'll work on a start-up project to come up with innovative ideas. Next, I'll spend seven weeks working on a client project, gaining experience working with external clients. Finally, I'll have the opportunity to explore a topic that interests me during the last five weeks.
I'll document my approach and results for each project to reflect on my progress, identify areas for improvement, and track my learning outcomes.

Start-up Project

week 1/2
For my first project in semester 6 of Media Design, I get to create a startup company with my group! I'm thrilled to start this exciting challenge that demands creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking. We will also receive feedback from Hike One, a UX Design company, through weekly meetings. With Hike-One's critical feedback and feedforward, we can refine our ideas and make progress towards creating a successful startup.
As a group we decided to use the Double Diamond for the design process, all of my group members are familiar with this design process and therefore it was an easy decision to go for that design process.

Brainstorming new ideas

Our journey to create a start-up began with the generation of ideas and concepts. To facilitate this process, we experimented with various brainstorming techniques, including Flower Association. This approach led us to come up with several innovative and creative ideas, many of which were focused on sustainability. After receiving feedback from our teachers, we narrowed down our list of ideas to two distinct and exciting concept:

Fontys Community:
An online community platform for students that allow them to communicate, host events, do user testing, and much more.

Ai Recipe Generator:
An application that can generate personalized recipes based of off user input.


At one point the majority of the group, including myself was really excited about the AI Recipe Generator, partialy due to the fact that the idea was generated by an AI, chatGPT and I have been really inspired by all of these new advancements in artificial intelligence.
During this time we also generated different names for the application as well with the help of chatGPT. We did this until we landed on: Recipeazy



"Recipeazy is an innovative cooking concept that focuses on personalizing recipes for all types of users. For many people, finding recipes that perfectly fit their personal needs and preferences can be a challenge. This is where Recipeazy provides a solution. By utilizing artificial intelligence, Recipeazy can generate personalized recipes based on the specific preferences of the user and cater to them. This provides an easy and unique way to prepare meals that fully match everyone's individual taste and preferences."
