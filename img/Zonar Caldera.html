Build a B(r)and

Project Description

For a starting band or artist (act) it is quite difficult to make a name in the music industry. To be successful in this industry an exceptional musical talent is not enough. In a group context, we worked on an assignment named "Build a B (r) and). For a client, an artist or a band, we worked on the brand, the identity of the band and the user experience that will result in associated digital products. Our proces is based on iteration.

Sprint 0

In this first sprint we first had to understand who the Nachtwakers are. We created some Artistic Explorations and Brandguide Inspirations to fully understand the band.

The nachtwakers is a Ska/Regga band from Tilburg. They're in their final years of the Fontys Rock Academy. Like the name implies, they always play and make music at night.

I placed some images together that fit best with the band.

Artistic Exploration Research

Sprint 1

End Reflection

Starting the education I knew nothing about coding or webdesign. When I was younger I always collected logos and glued them on a poster. I ended up framing this an putting it on the wall. I never knew I would actually like to design media products.

Learning how to code in HTML/CSS and Javascrpit turned out to be quite difficult, but I managed to understand most of it and I can confidently say that I can build websites. Coding wasn't really something I was really interested in, but now I really enjoy doing it.

In the past I did work with Windows Movie Maker to edit videos, but nothing compares to Adobe Premiere Pro. Making skateboard videos thought me so many things. Things like keyframes and colorcorrection. Designing media products was actually pretty fun. I never thought I would be designing logos and posters.

I really enjoyed this semester and I am curious what I will bring to the table next semester.
