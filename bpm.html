Introduction


Based on the motivations behind going to a festival that I learned from my research and my thoughts on interactions, I started developing another concept next to the Conversation Sparkers. This concept will fullfil the intrinsic rewards in a different way that the Conversation Sparkers did. During the process of creating this concept, I kept the key points of my vision in mind, and created a design that not too distracting to the preformance.

The feeling of belonging to a bigger group, part of a large crowd of like-minded people, is one of the many interactions that we seek, when going to a festival. A simple 'Chatbooth' can give the visitors a chance to meet their favorite artist and give them that reward of 'being part of a larger crowd' or be in the presensce of someone that they value very highly.

I decided to work on this concept that allows people to interact with the artist, but in a different way. These are the following elements I focussed on:

- The need for belonging to large crowd
- Interact with the artist in a different way
- Let the online visitors interact with the real world

Interaction
Next to the interaction between the visitors there is also interaction between the preformers and festival visitors. There are many way to allow this interaction to happen. This can be distracting to some preformers, but it can also be rewarding to both parties. Think of the preformers asking the audience to sing along and then point the microphone towards them. That moment when you and your friends appear on the big screens at the festival, this can be a way to create an opportunity to allow interaction to happen between the visitors and the interaction between the visitors and preformers.

Phase one


What if a festival or concert allowed the audience to project their own content on a big screen by using their phone camera.
Of course, this could lead to problems, like people misusing this and ruining the entire festival experience.
But what if you could allow the audience to project preselected stickers or animations on the preformers? The details and specifics of these stickers or animations can be discussed with the artist and the Effenaar.

First Version

Implementation
Think of it as a Snapchat lens but then it is projected on the preformers with a projector. This can be done with "Body Projection Mapping", this is a technique that projects animations on top of a person and the projection moves along with the person as well.
How could I recreate this?
In order to project on top of a person, the projector needs to understand where to project. This can be done with a simple camera in combination with body detection software. Once the software understands where the person is in relation with the projection, it can neatly cut out the shape of a live preformer.
Tech Needed
