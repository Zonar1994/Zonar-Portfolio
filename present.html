Portfolio I used this portfolio, to show the work I did over the last semester. Personally, I find it quite visually appealing. I am especially fond of my filtration system on the home page. However, I did realize, that the current format requires a lot more reflecting and explanation than I would like for my future projects. Based on previous feedback and some introspection, I know that writing down and properly formulating my progress in text is something I struggle with. Even though I struggle with putting it into text, I do not feel like this represents the actual quality of my work or my growth towards my learning goals.

This semesters, I asked for help on how to present my work in text. By speaking trough all the work I have done to people around me, it allowed myself to visualize a timeline and connect my actions to my learning goals. In the future I want to avoid having to deal with so much text in general, and would like to do a different aproach. An idea I have in mind at the moment, is a presentation via video. But this is a topic I would like to thinker upon more, once it comes to making my next portfolio. Other forms of presenting

  • Posters
  • Videos
  • Exhibitions (EXPO)


Duo Project
Looking back on my duo project, the Neural home and my client project with the Effenaar, specially the concept Conversation Sparkers, I concluded that the following projects best show my growth on this learning outcome.

With the DUO project, I presented the first pitch of the Neural Home project. I created a presentation that explained our vision, technology and future development.
The first poster I created for the Neural Home, was to give users a simple yet comprehensive explanation on how the Neural Home automation worked. The second poster for the Neural Home was created to draw attention to my project. It showed the target group interacting with a similar device and the functionalities of the application.

Both of these posters use the design colours of Emotive headsets, the brand we used to create our applications with, as a colour pallet and were showcased at the DUO project EXPO.


I also created a video showcasing all the interaction possibilities with the Neural Home application. This showcases how the user interacts with the Neural Home application and was made with Adobe XD.






Effenaar
I presented the first pitch of the ideas of the client project. With the help of a Miroboard I showed the progress of our project and our future developments. Next to that I also presented the final prototypes at the exposition that Jos from the Effenaar also attended.



The posters for the Conversation Sparkers were visuals that were used to draw attention of the visitors at the EXPO. The posters explain the functionalities of the Conversation Sparkers and are in the style of Hit The City.
Next to a static image of the Conversation Sparkers poster, I also created an animated one. This shows animated shapes in a visually appealing way, drawing the visitors of the exposition. This is not something I had previously experimented with and was made with Adobe XD.
In order to show all of the iterations at the exposition, I created an iteration board that showcased all of phases and iterations of my client project and correlated this to the phases I reflected on in my portfolio.
