Societal Impact

Self assessment: Undefined

The student understands the various angles from which their AI projects can have a sustainable impact on society. They're also conscious of the legal aspects of data use and the potential societal repercussions.

Since the start of ChatGPT and Dall-e, I have been fascinated with the possibilities that AI can offer. I've always thought of it as a Pandora's box that the human race created. It holds the power to change civilizations as we know them, but it also contains utterly unimaginable consequences that could end them. I care deeply about the impact of AI and am constantly thinking about ensuring that its use is ethical and aligns with our morals and values.

Investigative Problem Solving

Self assessment: Undefined

The student can scrutinize their AI projects from multiple viewpoints, identify shortcomings, and develop fitting solutions.

I am planning to run experiments to make language models better at problem-solving. The idea is to identify what's lacking in existing models and find ways to improve that. This should help the AI to think more clearly and make smarter choices. While making the models smarter is exciting, it's also key to keep them easy to understand and use.

Data Preparation

These experiences have underlined the paramount importance of meticulous data preparation in achieving accurate and reliable outcomes.
