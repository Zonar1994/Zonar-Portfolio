Investigative Problem Solving

Purpose

To enhance AI's problem-solving abilities by enabling a more human-like approach to decision-making, where AI can consider different options before making decisions.

How It Was Done

Developed and addressed "How Might We" questions to drive the exploration of innovative solutions in AI. For example, to tackle model explainability in AI, I investigated and applied the "Tree of Thoughts" methodology. This approach diverges from traditional linear AI processes, creating a more effective way for AI to handle complex tasks.

Societal Impact

By improving AI's ability to solve problems through enhanced explainability, we contribute to various sectors by providing more effective and useful AI technologies. This ultimately aids society by making complex AI more accessible and trustworthy.
