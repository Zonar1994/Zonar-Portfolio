Group Project: Enhancing the LAIO system | Journalism

Introduction

Our project, a collaboration with Innovative Origins, embarked on enhancing the existing AI system responsible for generating news articles. Our diverse team, comprising talents from software development, AI, cybersecurity, media design, and business economics, was asked to enhance the system's fact-checking capabilities, thereby elevating the reliability of content produced.

Project Planning Phases

We began by establishing a clear proposal that defined our objectives. The main goal was to build an AI component that could analyze text and distinguish facts from opinions, aiding journalists in producing articles more efficiently.

  • Discover Phase: Worked together with Journalist to generate news articles with AI tools and created a demonstration video for that. Initial research involved studying LLMs for fact-opinion differentiation and investigating various AI agents and methodologies like Langchain and LAIO.
  • Define Phase: We assessed the societal impact using a TICT scan and defined the technological needs to ensure ethical considerations were woven into our development process. We also worked on instructions for langchain agent, defining the flow of a journalist.
  • Develop Phase: The development phase was initiated with a prototype demonstration, with a set deadline for a working model by late November.
  • Deliver Phase: The concluding phase was dedicated to finalizing the prototype, documenting our findings, and outlining recommendations for the system’s future improvements.

Discover Phase: The world of Journalism

In a week-long session with journalists, we, a group of IT students, partnered with them to produce articles quickly. We used AI tools like ElevenLabs for voiceovers, ChatGPT for article writing, and AgentGPT for extra information. The journalists showed us how they work daily, giving us useful tips on using AI in their field. Together, we made a demo video about a made-up bombing in the Netherlands, demonstrating how AI can help in making news stories by turning the latest info into a narrated report. This showed us that journalists could use better tools for quick research, and it guided us in making AI tools to help them gather the latest news faster.

    Reflection

    Personal Growth and Learning:
     My involvement in the project significantly enhanced my understanding of Langchain agents and n8n, opening up new perspectives on the potential impact of these tools in journalism. I discovered how they could revolutionize journalistic work by facilitating efficient research and factual article generation. The project also led me to ponder deeper philosophical questions about the nature of news and facts.

    Project Outcomes:
     I’m pleased with the outcomes of our project. While it involved a lot of research and prompt engineering, we created a sophisticated yet accessible system. The challenge lay in translating journalistic knowledge into instructions for an AI agent, delving into the cognitive and problem-solving aspects of LLMs. The positive reactions from others were inspiring, highlighting the potential impact of our work on the journalistic community and beyond.

    Team Dynamics and Role:
    Our team had a light-hearted and enjoyable dynamic, with everything generally proceeding as planned, despite some delays in development and planning. I naturally assumed a leadership role, drawing on my AI knowledge to guide the project and create plans. Our collaborative efforts eventually led to the successful completion of our project.

    Methodology and Approach:
    Employing the Double Diamond method proved effective, providing structure and direction once we gained momentum. This approach was key to our project’s progress and eventual success.

    Client and Stakeholder Engagement:
    Interaction with our client started slowly but improved over time. The most valuable insights came from journalists, both at the Innovative Insights event and in direct conversations. Their feedback, particularly regarding the importance of visual elements in journalism, was enlightening.

    Retrospective Insights:
    If I were to revisit the project, I would focus more on in-depth research and enhancing the visual aspects, addressing journalists' feedback for more innovative solutions. My takeaway is the importance of adhering to a research methodology and establishing a clear plan early on. The experience with Langchain agents was invaluable, and I plan to integrate these tools into future projects wherever possible.
    Project Challenges and Areas for Improvement:
    While I am pleased with the overall outcome, the project did face its share of challenges. The development phase, in particular, was more time-consuming than anticipated. Establishing an effective project plan took longer than expected, which initially slowed our momentum. In retrospect, a more streamlined planning process and quicker decision-making could have accelerated our progress.
    Team Dynamics and Overcoming Obstacles:
    Our team dynamics were generally positive, but managing task durations and coordinating efforts effectively was an area of struggle. As an informal leader, guiding the team through these delays and ensuring we stayed on track was a learning experience. Balancing leadership with collaboration, especially when tasks took longer than planned, was a significant aspect of my role.F
