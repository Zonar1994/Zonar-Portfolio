Scope of the project

I conducted extensive research on various aspects of AI assistants for email processing. This research involved delving into topics such as computational trust models, user experience factors, societal implications, privacy concerns, and common sense in AI. After exploring these areas, I realized that creating a fully functional AI email assistant would be a challenging task. However, I recognized the potential for a great proof of concept. Therefore, I decided to focus on developing a prototype of an AI email assistant that can perform tasks like composing and sending emails, organizing incoming emails, suggesting replies, and providing reminders.

User Stories/b>

User stories, outlining user needs for an AI email assistant, guided my project's direction. These stories targeted actions like composing emails, categorizing messages, suggesting responses, setting reminders, and retrieving information. They shaped the prototype's design by defining its core functionalities and serving as benchmarks for successful implementation. These stories ensured a user-centric focus throughout my research, development, and reflection stages, aligning project outcomes with user needs, thereby improving the assistant's usability and effectiveness. In essence, they provided a structured, iterative approach to the project, maintaining a clear focus on enhancing the user experience.

Research Questions

  • How can AI assistants be trained to generate coherent and contextually accurate email responses, taking into account the content and context of the email conversation, to enhance the authenticity and value of communication?
  • What strategies and techniques can be employed to incorporate social norms and ethical considerations into AI assistants' email composition, ensuring respectful and appropriate interactions that align with users' personal or business communication styles?
  • How can natural language processing techniques be utilized to compose emails that are indistinguishable from human-composed emails in terms of style, tone, and content, thereby improving the communication experience for both the user and recipients?

AI Assistants for business

To better understand how an AI assistant can be utilized in a business setting, I conducted research by closely observing a consultancy business where I currently work as an AI researcher. This firsthand experience provided valuable insights into the specific needs and challenges faced by the business. Through techniques like discreet observation and actively participating in their tasks, I learned about their requirements for automation, the need for speed and efficiency, complex processes they handle, effective task management, and the importance of data security. These insights guided the development of an AI assistant prototype tailored to meet the consultancy industry's unique needs.

Autonomous and problem solving agents

In order for me to understand a LLM could possibly behave autonomously, in a way that it could solve problems and remove anyhallucinations, I set out to find out the Best, good & bad practices. in LLM's, espescially those with large training data. I explore the "Chain Of thought" and "Tree of Thought" reseach papers and try to gather insights on how I could create this AI assistant.

  • Chain-of-Thoughts:
    • Enhances reasoning capabilities of AI models.
    • Improves understanding of context in business scenarios.
    • Enhances advanced skills like arithmetic, commonsense, and symbolic reasoning.
    • Achieves high accuracy, crucial for effective decision-making.
  • Tree of Thoughts:
    • Improves decision-making by considering multiple reasoning paths.
    • Enhances problem-solving abilities, useful for troubleshooting and complex business problems.
    • Enables planning and search tasks, beneficial for project management and data analysis.
    • Facilitates adaptive learning and improvement of AI assistant's responses over time.
    • Enhances natural language processing for better understanding and response to user queries.
Overall, integrating these methodologies, such as Auto-GPT, can create more autonomous and capable problem-solving agents in various domains, benefiting businesses and improving AI assistant interactions. Responsible and ethical development is crucial for their successful implementation.

Common sense in LLM’s

To create an AI assistant, it is essential to consider key factors. Yejin Choi's research highlights the challenges of large language models (LLMs) and the importance of common sense. Integrating smaller, safer AI systems imbued with human norms and values can address these concerns. Theory of Mind (ToM) is crucial for developing assistants that understand user queries in a human-like manner. Evaluating ToM abilities through specific benchmarks and considering dataset difficulty levels enhances training. Incorporating common sense, transparency, and democratization creates intelligent, ethical, and reliable AI assistants.

The N8N Prototype

I developed a Chatbot Email Assistant to streamline email processing in organizations using n8n workflow automation and the CHATGPT API. The system categorizes incoming emails into HR, Marketing, Sales, or Finance departments using nodes I designed for tasks such as email receiving, department selection, and response generation. A unique dataset trained the AI assistant for each department. The prototype utilizes an Email trigger node with IMAP, which listens for incoming emails and enables the assistant to generate structured responses. Rigorous testing assured optimal model parameters, resulting in a reliable and efficient email processing system.
